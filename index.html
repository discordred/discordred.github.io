About Discord RED!

Discord RED is a guild focused on gaming and community. We pride ourselves on what Discord had in mind when it was developed. To be a free place for gamers and communities to hangout, game, talk and overall, have fun.

Rules of Discord RED!

The rules we have are relatively simple. We just ask you follow the rules and enjoy yourselves!
  1. Make sure to follow the Discord Terms of Service.
  2. The decisions of Moderators and Staff are final! (Moderators can be superceded by Staff)
  3. Don't spam, troll, make disturbances or advertise other Discords without consulting staff!
  4. Keep topics in the appropriate channels

The Channels of Discord RED!

We have channels designed for all peoples! If we don't then feel free to mention it to someone on staff and we can talk about it! If we agree or get enough feedback on it then we can surely make that happen!
